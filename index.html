Explore our Menu

Explore global food on our extensive menu. We provide distinctive flavors including Fish Tacos, Deep Dish Pizza, Fried Catfish, and Lobster Mac & Cheese. experiences. Taste South African Bobotie, North African Couscous, and West African Fufu and Soup. Chicken Fried Rice, Noodles with Meatballs, Spicy Citrus Noodles, Stir Fry Noodles, and Vegetable Dim Sum Soup bring Chinese harmony. Enjoy French elegance with Bisque, Bouillabaisse, Croque Monsieur, and Onion Soup. Savour Indian Butter Chicken, Dosa, Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani, Samosas, and Tandoori Roti. Explore Japanese art through Onigiri, Sushi, Takoyaki, Tempura, and Yakitori. Finally, try Green Curry, Pad Thai, Panang Curry, Spicy Green Papaya Salad, and Tom Yum soup. Our varied assortment of international delicacies pleases all tastes.
